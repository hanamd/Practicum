all involved stakeholders, and often takes on many

roles throughout the process in an advisory or lead

capacity to inform collection, data systems, data

mitigate bias and maximize effective outcomes

and peers, and more

and the needs of all involved stakeholders

and data-related business processes and their

stakeholder needs/requirements
